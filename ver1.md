# Version 1 (02/12/2025) – Feature Expansion Suggestions

## 1. UI / Layout Improvements
- Expand the website to fully utilize the screen size (full-width, full-height layout).
- Improve responsiveness across devices (desktop-first, tablet-friendly).

## 2. Earnings Calendar Integration
- Connect the platform to an **earnings calendar API**.
- Features to include:
  - Upcoming earnings notifications for selected tickers.
  - A **calendar-style UI** (monthly/weekly view) displaying earnings dates.
  - Ability to subscribe/unsubscribe from specific companies’ earnings alerts.

## 3. Watchlists
### 3.1 Permanent Market Watchlist
Include always-visible benchmark assets such as:
- **SPY / VOO** – ETFs tracking the S&P 500
- **DIA** – ETF tracking the Dow Jones Industrial Average
- **QQQ** – ETF tracking large-cap and growth-focused tech companies
- **NASDAQ Index**
- **NYSE Index**

### 3.2 Currency Watchlist
Track major FX pairs relevant to U.S. markets:
- USD → GBP
- USD → EUR

(Expandable later to include other major currencies.)

## 4. Stock Price Visualization
- Add **X and Y axes** to stock price charts:
  - X-axis: Time
  - Y-axis: Price
- Enable viewing price changes across multiple time horizons (intraday, 1D, 1W, 1M, 1Y).

## 5. Bond Market & Yield Curve
- Add a **daily-updating U.S. Treasury yield curve chart**.
- Purpose:
  - Visual summary of economic health and investor confidence.
  - Known historical patterns make it a strong recession indicator.
- Chart should display yields across different maturities (e.g., 3M, 2Y, 10Y, 30Y).

## 6. Housing Market (Separate Tab)
Because housing is a large domain, it should live in its **own dedicated tab**, separate from stocks, bonds, and crypto.

### 6.1 Housing Prices
- Display housing prices categorized by:
  - Property type (single-family, apartment, condo, villa, etc.)
  - Geographic location (city, state, region)

### 6.2 Housing Price Visualizations
- Include **average pricing graphs** over time.
- Enable comparison by location and property type.

### 6.3 Interest Rates & Fed Policy
- Display current **Federal Reserve interest rates**.
- Explain the connection between Fed rate changes and:
  - Commercial bank lending rates
  - Mortgage interest rates

### 6.4 Mortgage & Loan Health Metrics
- Track **mortgage delinquency rates** (monthly updates if possible).
- Optionally expand to general loan delinquency rates.
- Could live either:
  - Within the Housing tab, or
  - In a broader **Economic Indicators** tab

## 7. Optional Economic Indicators (Future Consideration)
- Inflation metrics
- Employment data
- Consumer confidence

*(These can be added later or omitted depending on scope.)*

---

> All points above are suggestions and can be prioritized or phased based on development scope and data availability.
